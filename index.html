Who Are We?

Our Mission

Through accessible information, interactive tools, and innovative smart shelter technology, MIAO empowers communities to understand, protect, and care for stray feline populations. By combining compassion with data-driven solutions, we aim to build a more humane and connected urban environment—for cats and for us all.

Our Services

We offer interactive tools and informative articles to spread awareness about the stray cat community. We also developed an innovative smart cat shelter prototype that can be used to protect and monitor stray cats that need our special attention.

TNR -- What Is It, and Why Do We Need It?

Written By Letitia Lai

And then there’s a screech that arises from the night. Followed by another, and perhaps another. Rest assured—it is more likely that it is mating season for the cats than there is a ghost in your neighborhood.

Spanning from February to October, mating season for cats is a period in which female, called the queen, will go into heat multiple times from rising levels of the hormone estrogen until end of breeding season

Standing for Trap, Neuter, and Release, the TNR process is a system of stages in both neutering the stray cat and ensuring a smooth transition back to their original environment.

Components

  • ESP32-CAM
  • Arduino UNO
  • Type-A USB Cable
  • Male-to-Female Dupont Wires
  • DHT11 Temperature and Humidity Sensor
  • HX711 Weight Sensor
  • SG90 9g 180° Servo
  • Water Depth Detector
  • IIC OLED Display
  • Water Pump
  • Stainless Steel Bowl
  • Cushion
  • 3L Plastic Container

Key Functions

  • Real-time water level monitoring
  • Automatic water refill when level is low
  • Real-time temperature and humidity monitoring
  • Real-time cat food quantity monitoring
  • Automatic food replenishment when supply is low
  • Temperature and humidity monitoring and display
  • Real-time camera surveillance of cat activities

Designing Process

Exterior Designing Process

  • Drafting sketches
  • Preliminary dimension calculations and cutting of PVC foam boards
  • Assembly using a hot glue gun
  • Adding structural reinforcements for stability and load-bearing capacity
  • Ensuring partial detachability for future development

Implementation of Water and Food Dispensing Functions

  • Repurposing cut plastic bottles as water and food containers
  • Fabricating additional components (e.g., rotating discs, food chutes)
  • Developing Arduino C control programs
  • Parameter adjustment

Camera-Related Development Process

  • Initial attempt at cat face recognition:
    • Trial deployment of existing solutions
      • Failed due to legacy environment setup issues
    • Attempted alternative solutions
      • Failed due to insufficient data and environment configuration challenges
  • Reassessment of objectives under practical constraints:
    • Prioritized monitoring functionality over recognition
    • Adopted an IoT camera solution
  • Implemented a LANm-based remote monitoring solution
  • Explored intranet penetration as an extension:
    • Researched and tested mainstream intranet penetration solutions
    • Identified limitations of existing simple solutions:
      • Failed due to display logic constraints in local network monitoring
    • Evaluated alternative intranet penetration solutions for future consideration
